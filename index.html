Services

M/s. abc Marine Pvt. Ltd., renders the following services in her capacity as Service providers / consolidator

1. LOGISTICS CHAIN

(A) MANAGING THE PROCESS

M/s. abc Marine Pvt. Ltd., chain management services are designed to ensure that each step of your logistics chain / Port related services is carefully coordinated and managed. Our staff is comprised of professionals who are committed to securing the process so as to meet your requirements.

(B) LINKING UP WITH YOU In order to better understand your specific needs and expectations, we work closely together with you. We begin by designating a dedicated client team with the exclusive task of examining each step in your logistics chain / Port related services. Their job is to understand the nature of your business and assist in optimizing your processes. Working in conjunction with you, the client team will develop, implement and maintain standard operating procedures that will suit your specific needs, thus becoming the fundamental document upon which we will operate your logistics chain / Port related services.

2. Handling of Shipments

TIMING IS EVERYTHING Where time is of essence for certain shipments, in port is the natural choice. M/s. abc Marine Pvt. Ltd., understands why valuable time must not be lost. Our highly skilled employees care about your merchandise as much as you do. Loading / Un-loading of cargo detail of every stage, so your merchandise arrives / discharges safely and as quickly as possible. That includes processing all the administration work, handling and Loading / Un-loading before and after the Vessel / Barges in accordance with in total HSE compliance and Port regulations. We know that customer success and satisfaction stems not only from our solutions, but also from our staff. At abc Marine Pvt. Ltd., we understand that the punctual, safe and cost-effective Loading / Un-loading / delivery of all your cargo consignments is a fundamental aim of any Port Service provider organization. Whatever your shipment, urgent, high value or outsize cargo, our commitment to you is to provide the best solution for all your cargo needs. THE BENEFITS TO YOU We provide a 24 hour, 366 days a year service, ensuring a total commitment to our customers. Our expert knowledge combined with our comprehensive Port related services means you get the optimum solution to all your requirements. Our staff has extensive operational experience and is able to provide effective support and advice at every stage of the operation. CONSOLIDATION This is by far the most popular and cost-effective way to effect all the required movement in Mumbai Port. We facilitate our customers by making cargo consolidation by Sea which includes the transportation of cargo to the stuffing point, stuffing the cargo onto the Vessel / Barges, and make sure that the cargo will reach the respective destination well before the stipulated time given by the reputed customer. NOTE PL M/s. abc Marine Pvt. Ltd., will render all the port related services whereas M/s. MILLENIUM MARINE ENTERPRISES, will clear all the Scrap material arriving on barges/vessels at Mumbai Port/Anchorage from Mumbai High and Sludge-Used Oil will be cleared by our sister concern company M/s. abc Petrochem Pvt.Ltd.
